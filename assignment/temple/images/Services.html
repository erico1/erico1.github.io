Our Services

We believe that families are forever and we strongly support the upliftment, and the spiritual blessing that one can get from attending the temple, that's why on Temple Inn & Suites, we try as much as we can to provide you with the best customer service that will enhance both your spiritual and physical well-being. Below are some of the services we offer;

Services and Features

  • Free access to changing rooms and bathroom facilities for temple patrons who need to change to and from church clothes
  • Day-care room and low cost, short-term babysitting services for the children of patrons
  • Long term stay accommodations in kitchenette suites for full-time temple missionaries
  • Temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • Wedding reception hall that can be easily customized with a simple catering menu and kitchen access for food preparation and serving
  • Business center that supports family search and ordinance work

Amenities

  • Breakfast
  • Exercise/workout room
  • Indoor pool
  • WiFi high-speed internet access and smart tvs
  • Parking
  • Family history consultation and family ordinance card services
